Dr. Raphaël BLEUSE

• Dr. Raphaël BLEUSE is a research associate at Université du Luxembourg

Raphaël BLEUSE holds a Ph.D. degree in Computer Science from Univ. Grenoble Alpes, and a M.Sc. degree in Computer Science from Grenoble INP, France.

His research focuses on the design of low complexity scheduling algorithms for extreme scale parallel machines. His work covers both the theoretical aspects (complexity study, modelization of machines) and the practical aspects (implementation, performance evaluation through simulation and experimentation).

Alongside his research activities, he is coordinating the development of the Master "Smart Secure ICT for Business Innovation".

“This program provides a hollistic view on huge technological challenges faced by companies nowadays from an academic, industrial and standardization point of views. We believe this will bring not only a way to be proactive but also new business opportunities”

Prof. Pascal Bouvry, Principle Investigator
